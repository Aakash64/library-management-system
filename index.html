About Us

SVIT offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology disciplines.
The college is affiliated with the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Hyderabad, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
The courses at SVIT are designed to provide a strong foundation in theoretical knowledge while emphasizing practical application and industry-oriented training.
